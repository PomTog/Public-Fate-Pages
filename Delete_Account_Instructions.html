Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go! Follow these simple steps to permanently delete your Fate account.

How to Delete Your Account

1
Open the Fate app on your device
2
Tap on your Profile
3
Go to Settings
4
Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
5
Confirm your decision when prompted

Important Information

This action cannot be undone. When you delete your account:

  • All your profile information will be permanently removed
  • Your matches and conversations will be deleted
  • You will no longer appear in other users' feeds

Need Help?

If you're unable to access the app or need assistance with account deletion, please contact fate support at fate.userhelp@gmail.com

We're here to help and will respond as quickly as possible.
